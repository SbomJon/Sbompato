Deconstructing Social Housing

Created to be an inviting, safe and affordable home for people in need in our local neighbourhood, this project attempts to deconstruct what we take for granted about social housing.

I firmly believe that Social Housing should not be limited to dull, uninspiring buildings, so with this vision in mind, I designed a building in which the flats themselves would shape the façade of the building, each adorned with different vibrant colours to brighten up the grey surroundings.
